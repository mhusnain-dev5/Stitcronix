About Stitchronix

Your Trusted Manufacturing Partner in Pakistan

Stitchronix Apparel is a custom clothing manufacturing company based in Lahore, Pakistan. We specialize in producing high quality garments for startups, fashion brands, wholesalers and international clients.

Our focus is on quality craftsmanship, competitive pricing and reliable production. We provide complete apparel manufacturing solutions from sampling to bulk production while maintaining strict quality standards at every step.

How We Work

Our Manufacturing Process

A streamlined, transparent 6-step process from your first enquiry to final delivery at your doorstep.

💬 Step 01

Consultation

You share your requirements product type, quantity, design, fabric and budget.

📋 Step 02

Quotation

We prepare a detailed costing sheet with timeline, MOQ and fabric options.

🧵 Step 03

Sampling

Pre-production sample created for your approval before bulk production begins.

🏗️ Step 04

Production

Skilled team begins bulk manufacturing with strict quality checks at each stage.

✅ Step 05

Quality Control

100% inspection against your specifications measurements, stitching, print and finish.

🚢 Step 06

Packing & Delivery

Professional packaging with your branding, then shipped via air or sea worldwide.
