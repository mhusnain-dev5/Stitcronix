About Stitchronix

Your Trusted Manufacturing Partner in Pakistan

Stitchronix Apparel is a custom clothing manufacturing company based in Lahore, Pakistan. We specialize in producing high quality garments for startups, fashion brands, wholesalers and international clients.

ODM Manufacturing

How We Work

Our Manufacturing Process

A structured, enterprise-grade workflow from initial inquiry to customs-cleared delivery — built for precision at every step.

100% QC Pass Rate
24hr Quote Turnaround
Zero Hidden Costs
01
Inquiry Phase

Initial Consultation & Brief

Submit your garment brief — product category, target quantities, fabric preferences, market and target cost per unit. Our account managers respond within 24 hours with a structured intake form.

Structured Project Brief Document
02
Costing Phase

Detailed Quotation & Fabric Sourcing

We prepare a comprehensive costing sheet including unit pricing per MOQ tier, fabric specifications with GSM and composition, full production lead time, and shipping cost estimates to your destination port.

Factory Costing Sheet + Fabric Swatch Report
03
Development Phase

Pre-Production Sampling

A physical pre-production sample is crafted using the confirmed fabric, pattern and technical spec. You receive photos and measurement charts for review. Physical samples can be couriered on request.

Physical Sample + Measurement Audit Sheet
04
Production Phase

Bulk Manufacturing

Upon sample sign-off and deposit confirmation, we enter bulk production. Our floor supervisors conduct inline quality checks at cutting, stitching, and finishing stages. Weekly progress updates are shared.

Weekly Production Progress Reports
05
Quality Phase

Final QC & Compliance Audit

Every finished garment passes a 12-point quality inspection protocol covering measurements, stitching tension, print registration, color consistency, and finishing. AQL 2.5 inspection standards available on request.

12-Point QC Report + AQL Certificate
06
Dispatch Phase

Packaging & Global Export

Garments are packed as per your brand spec — individual poly bags, carton packing with size ratio manifests, and barcode compliance if required. We handle full export documentation, freight booking, and DHL/FedEx express options.

Export Invoice + Bill of Lading + Packing List
